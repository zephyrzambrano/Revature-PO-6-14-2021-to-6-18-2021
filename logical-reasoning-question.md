# Logical Reasoning Question

## Question

Direction Sense Test: Peter is in the East of Tom and Tom is in the North of John. Mike is in the South of John then in which direction of Peter is Mike?

South-East
South-West
South
North-East

## Answer

### Compass

    North

West        East

    South

### Layout of People

    Tom     Peter

    John

    Mike

### Answer

Mike is South-West of Peter
